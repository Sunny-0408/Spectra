🗺️ Treasure Hunt – Rules & Structure

📋 General Rules:

  • Each team must consist of 3–4 members.
  • Teams must stick together during the hunt – no splitting.
  • Use of mobile phones is not allowed unless specified.
  • Each round will eliminate teams with the lowest score or incomplete tasks.

🔍 Round 1: Clue Chase

  • Teams solve written puzzles to find locations.
  • Time-bound round – speed and accuracy matter.
  • Top teams move forward based on points.

🧭 Round 2: Code Break

  • Hidden codes at each checkpoint must be deciphered.
  • Tasks involve logic, observation, and collaboration.

🏁 Round 3: Final Treasure

  • All clues converge to one final treasure location.
  • First team to locate and claim the treasure wins.
  • Bonus points for creativity and teamwork.

⚠️ Disqualification Criteria:

  • Tampering with or hiding clues.
  • Misconduct or cheating in any form.
  • Disrespecting event coordinators or fellow participants.
